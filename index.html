About the Club

Crown & Quiver Archery Club is a community of young women who are passionate about archery, personal growth, and teamwork. Inspired by courage, confidence, and determination, our club provides a welcoming environment where members can develop their archery skills while building leadership, discipline, and lasting friendships.

Whether you're a beginner picking up a bow for the first time or an experienced archer looking to improve, Crown & Quiver encourages every member to aim high, challenge themselves, and grow both on and off the range. Through training sessions, friendly competitions, workshops, and social events, we strive to empower girls to become confident, focused, and resilient individuals.

Mission

"At Crown & Quiver Archery Club, we aim with grace, lead with courage, and stand together with strength. Our mission is to create a supportive community where young women can build confidence, develop leadership skills, and achieve their goals through the discipline and spirit of archery."

Vision

"Crown & Quiver aspires to be APU's leading girls' archery club, empowering young women to lead with courage, discipline, and resilience through the sport of archery."

Our Objectives

  • Empower girls through archery by building confidence, courage, resilience, and self-esteem in a supportive community.
  • Develop discipline, patience, perseverance, and responsibility through regular training, supporting academic and personal growth.
  • Represent APU in inter-university archery competitions and sporting events.
  • Provide consistent, structured training sessions that help members progress from beginner to competitive level.

Activities & Training

Lesson Timetable

Weekly training sessions, Tuesdays & Thursdays, 5–7 PM at the APU Sports Court. Beginner-friendly — equipment provided.

Ongoing Activities

Skill-building drills, technique workshops, and social bonding events that keep members improving and connected throughout the semester.

Competitions

Friendly internal matches each semester, plus opportunities to represent APU at inter-university archery competitions.

Rules & Safety

Safety on the range comes first — every member is expected to know and follow these rules at all times.

  • Members must follow all instructions given by coaches and safety officers at all times.
  • Proper safety gear must be worn during all training sessions (arm guard, finger tab, etc.).
  • Bows and arrows must only be handled on the shooting line or under supervision.
  • Never point a bow or arrow at anyone, even if it is not loaded.
  • Always wait for the signal before shooting or retrieving arrows.
  • No running, pushing, or horseplay is allowed in the range area.
  • Members must check equipment before and after use and report any damage immediately.
  • Respect all members, coaches, and equipment at all times.

Attire & Equipment

Attire

Comfortable fitted sportswear, closed-toe shoes, hair tied back, and no dangling jewellery or accessories.

Safety Equipment

Arm guard, finger tab or shooting glove, and chest guard (recommended) — provided for beginners.

Archery Gear

Recurve bow (club-approved), arrows, quiver, and a water bottle for hydration.
